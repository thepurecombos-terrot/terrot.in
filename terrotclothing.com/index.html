WELCOME TO TERROT

The Terrot is a leading customized uniform manufacturer and exporter of School Uniforms, Industrial Uniforms, Facility Management Uniforms, Hospital Uniforms, Hotel Uniforms, Salon & Spa Uniforms, Shirts, Pants, and T-shirts. We are located in Tirupur and Bangalore.

Founder Mr. Kannan SAA has 20 years of experience in fabrics, garment making, procurement of trims and machinery, merchandising, marketing, spinning, dyeing, weaving, and garment making companies. Presently, he takes care of marketing and monitors all other activities.

Our experience and expertise have helped the company grow from strength to strength by meeting the right quality requirements of buyers.

We produce a wide range of stylish and trendy uniform lines using the finest quality fabrics. Apart from school uniforms, our collections include men’s work wear and women’s work wear. Products are available in a wide range of colors, designs, and styles, and we also customize as per specific client requirements.

We aim to provide superior quality products that deliver high performance, ensured by stringent quality checks. Our team of designers, business-oriented professionals, and quality-conscious management has always been the backbone of our strength.

With over 25 years of industry experience, our ability to understand varying requirements and technical know-how has been key to our success. Many of our senior staff have been with us for more than a decade, ensuring stability in production and quality.

Our commitment is to modernize whenever possible, producing immaculate uniforms with innovative designs and persistent effort. As a leading uniform manufacturer, we maintain high international standards.

We look forward to your views, comments, and cooperation in business.
